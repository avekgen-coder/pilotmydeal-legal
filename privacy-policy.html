Privacy Policy

Pilot My Deal  ·  Effective date: July 9, 2026  ·  Provider: Yevgeniy Krupitskiy

The short version: Pilot My Deal was designed to know as little about you as possible. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and we never ask for your name, email, phone number, or location. The deals you analyze are stored on your device, not on our servers. We cannot see your deals.

1. Information Stored on Your Device Only

The App stores the following locally on your device. We do not receive, access, or transmit this information:

  • Deal inputs (purchase prices, rehab budgets, rents, financing terms, tax figures, and all other numbers you enter)
  • Saved deals and the names you give them
  • Free-text deal notes (anything you type, which may include property addresses or other details you choose to enter)
  • Your app settings, selected state, trial start date, subscription status flag, and the timestamp of your acceptance of these Terms of Use

Deleting the App from your device permanently deletes all of this data. We have no copy and cannot recover it for you.

2. Information We Receive

Anonymous device identifier (launch offers only). To run limited launch promotions fairly (e.g., "first 50 subscribers get 3 months free"), the App generates a random identifier on your device — a string of random characters that contains no personal information and is not derived from your name, Apple ID, phone number, advertising ID, or hardware serial number. When you claim a launch offer, this identifier and your assigned subscriber number are stored on our promotion server so the same device cannot claim two spots. That is the identifier's only purpose. We cannot use it to identify you.

Server connection data. Like virtually all internet services, our promotion server technically receives your IP address when the App checks offer availability. We do not log, store, or use IP addresses for any purpose.

3. Information Processed by Apple (Not by Us)

All purchases, free trials, and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple — not us — collects and processes your payment information, Apple ID, and billing details under Apple's Privacy Policy. We never see your payment card, bank details, or Apple ID.

To manage subscription entitlements, the App uses RevenueCat, a subscription-management service. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app-user identifier and purchase receipt data (product purchased, purchase date, renewal status) from Apple — not your name or payment details. RevenueCat processes this data on our behalf under RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not require or collect accounts, names, emails, phone numbers, or passwords
  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or files
  • We do not use advertising networks or show ads
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not "track" you across other companies' apps or websites (as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework)

5. Data Retention

Deal data and settings exist only on your device and are deleted when you delete the App. The anonymous launch-offer identifier is retained on our promotion server only while the promotion is active; once it closes, identifiers are deleted. We retain no other personal data.

6. Third-Party Services Summary

  • Apple App Store — app distribution, payments, subscriptions
  • RevenueCat — subscription entitlement management (anonymous IDs and receipts only)
  • Cloudflare — hosting for the launch-offer promotion server

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (including under the California Consumer Privacy Act and similar U.S. state privacy laws, and the GDPR/UK GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or obtain a copy of personal information. Because we hold almost none, most of these rights are satisfied automatically — but you may exercise any of them by emailing avekgen@gmail.com. We will respond within the time required by applicable law.

8. Security

The limited data we handle is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). The most sensitive information — your deals and notes — never leaves your device in the first place, which is the strongest protection available.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for adults evaluating real estate investments and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, contact avekgen@gmail.com and we will delete it.

10. International Users

The App and its promotion server are operated from the United States. If you use the App from outside the U.S., the minimal data described above is processed in the U.S., which may have different data-protection laws than your jurisdiction.

11. Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change, we will update this policy, revise the effective date, and provide notice in the App before material changes take effect.
