Pilot My Deal
Know the Best Move Before You Buy

Pilot My Deal is a real estate deal analyzer for iPhone. Run a Flip, BRRRR, Long-Term Rental, Medium-Term Rental, or Short-Term Rental analysis in seconds — no account required, all data stays on your device.

Subscriptions & Billing

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel or manage your subscription:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone
  • Tap your name → Subscriptions
  • Select Pilot My Deal and choose your option

For refund requests, contact Apple directly — subscription payments are processed by Apple and we do not have access to billing information.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is my data backed up? — Deals are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your data is permanently deleted. We recommend taking screenshots of important analyses.
  • What states are supported? — All 50 U.S. states, with state-specific transfer tax rates built in.
  • Is this financial advice? — No. Pilot My Deal is an educational calculator. Always verify figures with a licensed attorney, CPA, and lender before making any investment decision.
  • I lost my launch offer — can you restore it? — Launch offer codes are one-time use. If you have an unused code, redeem it in the App Store. Email us if you have questions.
